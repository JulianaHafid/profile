ASPIRING FULL STACK DEVELOPER

I am a mother of two but that should not stop me from following my passion. I love to code. Its exciting having to debug, fixing an issue. Sure enough, i cannot create buildings to live in but i can develop and contribute something on the web. Web is like part of our everyday life now. I can create something new that could help simplify and make our life better :)

I aspire to be a full stack developer. New technologies fascinates me. There is always something new to look out for. I love working on end to end web development. What you see on the surface sure is pretty but when you are able to make it work as a whole, its self fulfilment. It brings great satisfaction when i am able to deliver something of a good quality and as per project timeline.

During my free time, i usually unplug and hang out with my family. Now that i am growing older, i see the need to give it back to the community. I do volunteer work for the less fortunate children in a local children's hospital.
